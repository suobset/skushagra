Get to Know Me

The following is the personal website of Kush Srivastava: undergraduate CompSci major at UMass Amherst, graduating 2024. This homepage will cover the highlights of projects that I am working on currently. Complete details on everything that I am working on can be accessed via the About page. I also write stuff on here occassionally.

The reason behind creating this website has changed over the years: this domain went from being a website in High School teaching music to others, to being a portfolio website, to now furthering that and making this a repository for almost everything I do: creatively and professionally. I had created the last version of this website during Winter 2021, and that version over time felt constraining when it came to housing a full repository of everything I do (and not just a portfolio). I might keep using this home page area for such musings.

Currently, my main focus are in these endeavours; in-depth details and other current projects can be found under the Research page:

U.S. Census + UMass iCons TOP

Collaborating with a team of 6 UMass iCons students (myself included) on the "The Opportunity Project" by U.S. Census Bureau. Project proposal and guidelines by U.S. Department of Energy.

CICS Honors Thesis

Currently working with Prof. Joe Chiu and Prof. Tim Richards on my Honors Thesis pertaining to analysis of different programming languages at the Operating Systems level.

LinKaGe Lab @ Smith College

Working as a Research Assistant at the LinKaGe Lab under Dr. Ileana Streinu. Currently focusing on upkeep and development of different systems and software used by the lab.
