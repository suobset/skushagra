Get to Know Me

The following is the personal website of Kush Srivastava: undergraduate CompSci major at UMass Amherst, graduating 2024. This homepage will cover the highlights of projects that I am working on currently. Complete details on everything that I am working on can be accessed via the About page. Along with my portfolio, this website serves as my creative outlet (both, in and out of CompSci). The site logo is my first computer, circa 2013.

Currently, my main focus are in these endeavours; in-depth details and other current projects can be found under the Research page:

U.S. Census + UMass iCons TOP

Collaborating with a team of 6 UMass iCons students on the "U.S. Census Opportunity Project". Currently user-testing ASSERT: a GIS tool to provide aid to people overburdned by electrical outages.

CICS Honors Thesis

Currently working with Prof. Joe Chiu and Prof. Tim Richards on my Honors Thesis pertaining to analysis of different programming languages at the Operating Systems level.

LinKaGe Lab @ Smith College

Working as a Research Assistant at the LinKaGe Lab under Dr. Ileana Streinu. Currently focusing on upkeep and development of different systems and software used by the lab.
